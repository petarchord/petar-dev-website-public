Who is Petar?

Petar is a passionate and dedicated front-end enthusiast who enjoys building unique and fluent user expericences. His mission is clear - moving your business to the next level applying top-notch technologies.


He deeply believes that every problem is solvable and this is what drives him the most and make him go beyond the comfort zone in order to deliver extraordinary results.


He is an educated engineer with a bachelor degree in computer science from University of Nis, Serbia.


During his journey he had a chance to work in a different fields of software development including mobile development (Android Studio, Java), smart TV’s development (Tizen Studio, JavaScript), API development (NodeJS, JavaScript), which made him understand the bigger picture of software development, but he kept his focus on the front-end area as he feels most passionate about it.
